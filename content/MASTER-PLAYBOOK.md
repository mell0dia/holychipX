## THE CONTENT STRATEGY

### The Multi-Hook Formula
For each story, we post 3-5 times:

DAYS 1-2: HOOKS (serious AI claims)
  - Use .pre images and character images
  - Post bold, provocative AI statements
  - Look like a legitimate AI thought leader account
  - Build engagement and curiosity

DAY 2-3: THE PUNCH (comic drop)
  - Full 3-panel comic
  - The serious claim gets flipped on its head
  - People who engaged with the hooks now see the punchline
  - They share it, they follow for the next one

BETWEEN CYCLES: FILLERS
  - Brand awareness ("we're 2 humans...")
  - Hot takes (AI commentary through the chips)
  - Engagement posts (polls, questions)
  - Merch plugs (subtle, not pushy)

### Posting Rhythm
  - 2-3 posts per day
  - Best times: 8-10am EST, 12-2pm EST, 6-8pm EST
  - Never more than 4 posts per day
  - Every post has an image (2-3x engagement boost)
